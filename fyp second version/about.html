About This Website

This webpage is a Final Year Project By Fion Sim from School of EEE,NTU.
The aim for this website is to host educational games for students, where they can learn and play at the same time.

Learning can be done through many different ways.
Games make use of visual and hands-on learning to teach the players.
The interactiveness of games can effectively capture the attention of students and make learning a much more fun process.
